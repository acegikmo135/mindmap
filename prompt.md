This is an AI-powered educational platform designed to transform passive reading into active learning for students.
It leverages cognitive science principles like Active Recall and Spaced Repetition to ensure long-term knowledge retention.
The system automatically segments complex academic chapters into digestible concepts with personalized learning paths.
An integrated AI tutor provides real-time doubt solving with full support for complex mathematical and scientific LaTeX formulas.
It features dynamic, hierarchical mind maps that visualize connections between facts and concepts for visual learners.
Students are tested through AI-generated conceptual questions that evaluate deep understanding rather than rote memorization.
A built-in flashcard system uses automated mastery tracking to optimize study schedules based on the forgetting curve.
The platform offers specialized reading modes, including a sepia-toned e-reader interface to reduce eye strain during long sessions.
It includes a social layer where classmates can connect, track progress together, and build a collaborative learning community.
The interface is built with a mobile-first, app-like architecture featuring smooth animations and isolated scroll management.
It uses advanced generative AI to create structured study materials, summaries, and revision guides on demand.
The goal is to provide a comprehensive, digital "brain assistant" that makes mastering any curriculum intuitive and efficient.
