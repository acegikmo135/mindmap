What can you tell me about Wave-Particle Duality?

Markdown supported

Expert Explanation

Wave-particle duality is a fundamental principle of quantum mechanics which proposes that every particle or quantum entity may be described as either a particle or a wave. It addresses the inability of classical concepts to fully describe the behavior of quantum-scale objects.
