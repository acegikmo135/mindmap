That's one of the most profound questions in physics! It's not that the particle is "aware," but rather that "observation" in quantum mechanics requires an interaction.

Think of it this way: To see where a particle is, you have to bounce something off it (like a photon). That interaction is enough to knock the particle out of its delicate state of Superposition.
